Get the crew in one place.

Make a server for your crew and organize it into text and voice channels. Invite friends, set roles, and lock private channels to just the people you choose.

See

Clear voice chat with camera and screen sharing — show off a play, stream your game to the room, or just hang out face to face. Background blur included.

Play

Shared music with a live queue and DJ mode, Watch Together for synced video, plus party games like Sketch & Guess, Tic-Tac-Toe, polls, and a whiteboard.

Watch live streams together

Someone going live? Pull the stream up right inside your server and watch it as a group — live video from Twitch, YouTube, and Kik without anyone leaving the party. The Discover panel shows what's streaming so nobody misses the action.
